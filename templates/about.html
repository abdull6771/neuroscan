About NeuroScan

NeuroScan is a cutting-edge platform designed to assist medical professionals in the early detection of brain tumors using advanced deep learning technology. Our mission is to enhance diagnostic accuracy and improve patient outcomes through innovative AI solutions.

Developed by a team of AI researchers and healthcare experts, NeuroScan leverages a state-of-the-art TensorFlow Lite model trained on extensive MRI datasets. Our tool provides rapid, reliable classifications with confidence scores, making it an invaluable asset in clinical settings.

Our team is dedicated to advancing medical diagnostics.
