Meet Our CEO

Abdullahi Ahmad

CEO & Founder, NeuroScan

Abdullahi Ahmad is an innovative Computer Engineer and AI researcher from Babura, Jigawa, Nigeria, with over four years of experience in Machine Learning and Generative AI. He holds a Bachelor of Engineering (B.Eng.) in Computer Engineering from Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria, graduating with Second Class Upper honors, complemented by a Diploma in Computer Engineering from the same institution.

Professional Journey

Driven by a passion for artificial intelligence, Abdullahi specializes in Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), Large Language Models (LLMs), and workflow automation using tools like LangChain and LangGraph. His groundbreaking work earned him the Grand Prize at the Global Huawei ICT Competition (Innovation/AI Track) in 2023, recognizing his exceptional technical prowess and creativity.

As a Machine Learning Engineer Intern at Nafabat.AI, Abdullahi contributed to impactful projects, including fine-tuning LlamaVID for real-time shoplifting detection and developing end-to-end RAG applications. His academic research culminated in a smart waste robot using AI for waste classification and robotic disposal, presented as a poster at Ahmadu Bello University.

A sought-after speaker, Abdullahi has presented at national platforms like the Nigeria Computer Society AI Summit, showcasing assistive AI technologies for the visually impaired. He also mentors students and organizes workshops on AI and robotics to empower his community.
