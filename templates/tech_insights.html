Technology & Insights

Our Deep Learning Model

NeuroScan uses a convolutional neural network (CNN) optimized with TensorFlow Lite for efficient brain tumor classification. Trained on thousands of MRI scans, our model achieves high accuracy in detecting tumors with minimal computational overhead.

Performance Metrics

  • Accuracy: 92%
  • Sensitivity: 80%
  • Specificity: 94%
  • Inference Time: ~50ms on 2.4 GHz 8-Core Intel Core i9
